## üì± How to Access on Mobile

Your Stock Selection app is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that works seamlessly on mobile devices. It's designed with an iOS-inspired interface that looks and feels like a native mobile app.

### üåê Option 1: Access via Web Browser (Easiest)

1. **Get your Railway URL:**
   - Go to your Railway dashboard
   - Find your deployed service
   - Copy the public URL (e.g., `https://your-app-name.railway.app`)

2. **Open on Mobile:**
   - Open Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
   - Navigate to your Railway URL
   - The app will automatically adapt to mobile screen size

### üì≤ Option 2: Add to Home Screen (App-like Experience)

#### **On iOS (iPhone/iPad):**

1. Open Safari browser
2. Navigate to your Railway URL
3. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow) at the bottom
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Customize the name (e.g., "Stock Selection")
6. Tap **"Add"**
7. The app icon will appear on your home screen
8. Tap it to open in full-screen, app-like mode

#### **On Android:**

1. Open Chrome browser
2. Navigate to your Railway URL
3. Tap the **Menu** (three dots) in the top right
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
5. Confirm by tapping **"Add"** or **"Install"**
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen
7. Tap it to open in full-screen, app-like mode

### üîß Troubleshooting

**Issue: Can't see the sidebar**
- Tap the menu icon (‚ò∞) in the top left to open sidebar

**Issue: Buttons are too small**
- The app uses iOS-standard 44px touch targets
- If still having issues, try zooming out slightly

**Issue: App looks different on mobile**
- This is normal - the design adapts to mobile screens
- All functionality remains the same

**Issue: Can't add to home screen**
- Make sure you're using Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
- Some browsers don't support PWA installation
- You can still use it in the browser
